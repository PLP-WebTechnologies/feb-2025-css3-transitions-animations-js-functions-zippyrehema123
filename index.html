About Drug Abuse

Drug abuse affects people across all ages and backgrounds. It leads to health problems, addiction, and emotional distress. Awareness and education are the first steps to prevention.

You have the power to choose a better future. Stay drug-free and help others do the same.

Need Help?

If you or someone you know is struggling with substance abuse, reach out to a counselor or call a helpline in your area.
